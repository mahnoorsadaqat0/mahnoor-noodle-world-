Knorr Noodles Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Knorr noodles
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter
  • Optional: chopped vegetables (carrot, peas, capsicum)
  • Optional: chilli flakes, spring onion

Instructions

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan.
  2. Add noodles and cook for 2–3 minutes until soft.
  3. Drain most of the water leaving a little for softness.
  4. Add the masala (seasoning) from the packet and 1 tbsp oil/butter.
  5. Mix well. Add vegetables or garnish if desired. Serve hot.
